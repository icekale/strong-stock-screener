# Product

## Register

product

## Users

A-share short-term traders who maintain a focused stock pool, review strong-stock candidates, and need transparent data-source status before making manual decisions.

## Product Purpose

This is an independent strong-stock screening and watchlist management workbench. It helps the user screen candidates, inspect K-line and risk context, add stocks to structured watchlist groups, and track observation signals without mixing in daily market recap workflows.

## Brand Personality

Calm, disciplined, and data-forward. The interface should feel like a practical trading workbench: dense enough for repeated use, restrained enough that risk and signal labels remain legible.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page layouts, decorative hero sections, generic card dashboards, and any UI that makes this look like the abandoned daily-report project. Avoid hiding data-source or model state behind vague success labels.

## Design Principles

- Put workflow clarity before decoration.
- Keep screening, watchlist management, records, and settings visibly distinct.
- Make model outputs traceable with versions, source status, scores, and risk reasons.
- Preserve compact information density while keeping controls aligned and readable.
- Treat AI and paid data sources as decision support, not black-box trade instructions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use product-grade contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, and reduced-motion-safe interactions. Risk labels must not rely on color alone.
